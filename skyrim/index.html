Overview

Welcome to my Skyrim fan site! If you don't know what Skyrim is, it is an RPG game where you are a prison in an imperial town called Helgen. You are about to get your head chopped off to be executed, but a dragon comes out of nowhere over the city. You then escape the city with either Hadvar, or Ralof.

Dragon tongue

In Skyrim, the dragons have their own language, commonly known as Dovahzul. Unlike most movies with its own language, Skyrim has a full language, with over 500 words which you can actually learn yourself.

Shouts

Civil War

The civil war is a war between the Stormcloak rebellion, and the Imperial Legion. They are fighting for land, and what side becomes high king. You can join either side, by talking to a soldier, or one of the leaders of the Stormcloaks or Imperials.

Weapons

There are many weapons in skyrim, in different materials and types. Armor in skyrim works in a similar way.

Weapon types

There are many types of weapons in Skyrim. There are one handed weapons, and two handed weapons. One handed weapons include: Dagger, Sword, War axe, and Mace. Two handed weapons include: Greatsword, Battleaxe, and Warhammer. Other types of two-handed weapons include the Bow and arrows, and staffs.

Weapon speeds

Both one-handed weapons, and two-handed weapons have different speeds depending on what type of weapon it is. Daggers, Swords, War Axes, Maces, Greatswords, Battleaxes, and Warhammers is the order in how fast they swing respectively.
